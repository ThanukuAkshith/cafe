About The CAFE

The Greenhouse Café in Brooklyn offers American and Italian cuisine, known for its brunch and cozy ambiance, led by Executive Chef Robert Joseph. It features popular dishes like Eggs Benedict, Penne alla Vodka, and unlimited brunch cocktails. The Day Dream Cafe in Sheboygan Falls is a small, local café serving breakfast, lunch, coffee, and smoothies with rotating weekly specials. It doesn’t name a specific chef and appears to be run by a dedicated local team.
